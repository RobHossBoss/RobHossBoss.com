I am a student of Berea College class of 2017. I major in Computer Science with a concentration in Computational Mathematics. I am passionate about design and using mathematics to solve real problems. Whether I'm making the world more beautiful through design or providing value by solving a problem, I'm meticulous and creative.

I am very stoic and reserved. I am an INTJ after all. I spend +90% of my time alone, thinking, working, reflecting and planning. I am very deliberate in my actions. I prefer to get to know someone by working with them on a project or problem. When it comes to style, I prefer traditional and minimalist. I wear a button-up shirt, jeans, and a blazer every day. My desktop (computer and physical) is spotless.

Getting lost in a computer is one of my favorite ways to spend an afternoon. I'm not very active on social media but the occasional Reddit post or tweet can be expected. I like reading books about design and entrepreneurship. Check out my favorite books.

My favorite movies are what most people would probably consider weird but I like them because they explore ideas. I like music that has interesting sounds that I've never heard before (e.g. Trap, Techno, Dubstep, etc.). For background music, I like classical piano and Spanish guitar. Although, lately, I've been listening to podcasts more than music. Check out my favorite music and my favorite podcasts.

I drink my coffee black. Good coffee doesn't need cream and sugar. Tea is also a favorite, sometimes four cups a day! I enjoy cultural foods and am always trying new cuisines. Sushi for days!
