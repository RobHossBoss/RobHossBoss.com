About

Hi, I’m Robert. I’m a designer at heart and a programmer by nature. BluLocker

   BluLocker is a software tool that pairs your PC to your phone or other Blutooth capabe device. It locks your computer when your device goes out of range. Perfect for using your PC in a public place; never worry about people acessing your files when you are not present.

   BluLocker is currently coded in Python but I plan to rewrite it in Java once I am more capable in the language. BluLocker is my first attempt at a useful non-trivial programming project done outside of school.
